Introduction to Calculus: Limits and Continuity

Sarah Johnson May 15, 2024 45 minutes

Lecture Description

In this lecture, we'll explore the fundamental concepts of limits and continuity in calculus. You'll learn how to evaluate limits algebraically and graphically, understand the definition of continuity, and apply these concepts to solve real-world problems.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the concept of a limit and its notation
  • Evaluate limits using algebraic techniques
  • Identify points of discontinuity in functions
  • Apply the Intermediate Value Theorem
